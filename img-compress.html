How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compression tool helps you reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Select your desired output format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
  3. Adjust the quality slider to balance file size and image quality
  4. Optionally resize your image to specific dimensions
  5. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  6. Download your optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading: Smaller images load faster, improving user experience and SEO rankings
  • Reduced bandwidth usage: Save on hosting costs and data transfer
  • Better storage efficiency: Store more images in less space
  • Improved social media sharing: Many platforms compress images automatically, so better to optimize first
